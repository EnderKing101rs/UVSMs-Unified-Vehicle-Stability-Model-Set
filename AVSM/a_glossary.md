# AVSM Glossary: Air Vehicle Stability Model

The **AVSM** calculates the Stability Index ($S$) for aerial systems, swapping ground friction for aerodynamic lift and stall margins.

## The Master Equation (Air)

$$S_{Air} = \underbrace{\left[ \frac{L \cos\phi}{mg} + \mathcal{M}_{d} \right]}_{\text{Restoring}} + \underbrace{\left[ \rho (C_{L_{max}} - C_L) \right]}_{\text{Buffer}} - \underbrace{\left[ \sin\phi + \frac{\text{Gust}}{mg} \right]}_{\text{Toppling}} - \underbrace{k \left[ \frac{\Delta x_{CG} + \tau \dot{\Delta x}}{L_{ref}} \right]}_{\text{Penalty}} - \underbrace{\left[ \frac{K_{wing} \gamma^2}{2mg} \right]}_{\text{Shock}}$$

---

## Variable Definitions

### 1. Lift Restoring (The Machine's Will to Fly)
* **$L$**: Lift force generated by wings.
* **$\phi$**: Bank angle.
* **$\mathcal{M}_{d}$**: Dihedral Effect. The natural tendency of the wings to level themselves.

### 2. Stall Buffer (The Grip on the Air)
* **$\rho$**: Air Density.
* **$C_{L_{max}}$**: Maximum Lift Coefficient before a stall occurs.
* **$C_L$**: Current Lift Coefficient.

### 3. Toppling Forces (The Aerial Environment)
* **Gust**: Vertical or lateral wind shear attempting to upset the frame.

### 4. Shift Penalty (The Payload/Pilot Factor)
* **$\Delta x_{CG}$**: Center of Gravity Migration. Displacement of weight inside the cabin.
* **$\tau$**: Pilot Reaction Lag. The delay in stick input to correct a pitch/roll error.

### 5. Aeroelastic Shock (The Structural Limit)
* **$K_{wing}$**: Wing Spar Rigidity.
* **$\gamma$**: Wing Deflection (Flutter). The energy stored in the wing flex during high-G maneuvers.
